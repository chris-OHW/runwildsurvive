Privacy Policy

Run Wild Survive

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Run Wild Survive ("the App," "we," "us") handles information when you use our mobile application.

The Short Version

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The App runs entirely on your device. Nothing leaves it.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any identifying information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or gameplay statistics
  • Crash reports or error logs
  • Any data from your microphone, camera, or contacts

Data Stored Locally On Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only, using your device's standard local storage. This data never leaves your device and we cannot access it:

  • Game progress and high scores
  • Leaderboard initials you choose to enter
  • Which animals, costumes, and game modes you have unlocked
  • Purchase status for in-app purchases
  • App settings and preferences

You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing app data in your device settings.

In-App Purchases

The App offers in-app purchases including individual animals, costume packs, trail effects, and a VIP subscription. All purchases are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) — we do not handle, store, or process any payment information. Please refer to Apple's privacy policy for information about how they handle purchase data.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, social media platforms, or data brokers.

Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users, as described above.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing for us to provide, correct, or delete on your behalf. All locally stored game data can be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the App.
